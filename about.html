I am a B.Tech student in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, building expertise at the intersection of intelligent systems, scalable backend architectures, and data engineering. My work spans ML pipelines, full-stack applications, and production-quality Python engineering.

I don't just learn concepts — I build with them.
